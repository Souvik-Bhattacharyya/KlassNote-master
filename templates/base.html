Easy Note Stationary Private Limited

Established in the year 2009, Klassnote by Easy Note Stationary Private Ltd. has it's headquarters located in Kolkata. The company has grown to be East India's premier stationary brand and has a distributor network of more than 300 and growing. Klassnote supplies personalised exercise books to over 250 schools spanning across West Bengla, Orissa, Bihar, Jharkhand and NorthEast of India. Also, with over 170 different products which are associated with the brand name Klassnote they are soon to open their very first retial store in Kolkata.
